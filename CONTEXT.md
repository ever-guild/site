# Ever Guild Website

Public marketing site for Ever Guild — a senior engineering team. Presents capability and trust to founders and product leaders evaluating delivery partners.

## Language

**Ever Guild**:
A senior engineering team that builds and maintains production-grade software (Web3, AI, full-stack).
_Avoid_: Community, agency (on the public landing page)

**Team** (vs community):
The senior engineers visitors may work with directly. Primary identity on the landing page.
_Avoid_: Community (landing-page positioning; community language belongs on GitHub)

**Redesign scope**:
Visual identity refresh with palette and logo locked; typography, layout, surfaces, navigation chrome, card patterns, and motion are open to change.
_Avoid_: Full rebrand (new colors or mark), community-first landing

**Register**:
Brand/marketing — design carries the message; this is not a product UI shell.

**Atmosphere**:
No full-page 3D or particle background. The site uses flat, engineered surfaces; depth comes from typography, spacing, and surface hierarchy.
_Avoid_: WebGL hero fields, glass-over-particles, scroll-linked 3D ambience

**Team presentation**:
Real member photos, shown with a single unified visual treatment (consistent crop, frame, and color grading) so mismatched source shoots read as one system.
_Avoid_: Mixed ad-hoc photo styles, stock-looking portraits, glass card grids with inconsistent imagery

**Copy scope**:
Tighten existing messaging — keep core value props, remove redundant HUD/kicker duplication and template chrome, sharpen vague section leads. Not a full rewrite.
_Avoid_: Full copy overhaul without review, keeping duplicate fact blocks (Since 2021, remote-first, etc. in multiple places)

**Layout**:
Editorial, asymmetric — left-aligned hierarchy, strong vertical rhythm, varied section widths. Not a centered symmetric SaaS landing template.
_Avoid_: Centered hero stacks, identical full-width card grids, mirrored column layouts
