# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Ever Guild serves founders, product leaders, and engineering organizations that need senior software delivery without building a large permanent team first. Visitors arrive while evaluating whether the team can build, maintain, or rescue complex Web3, AI, and full-stack products.

## Product Purpose

The website presents Ever Guild as a senior engineering team for production-grade software. Success means visitors quickly understand the team's capability, trust its technical judgment, and can reach the right external channel or start a project.

## Brand Personality

Precise, senior, durable. The voice should feel technically fluent and direct, with confidence coming from specificity rather than hype.

## Anti-references

Avoid community-first language on the public landing page; community positioning belongs to the GitHub presence. Avoid generic SaaS hero tropes, decorative gradient text, identical card grids, excessive glassmorphism, sketchy illustrations, over-rounded cards, and vague claims that do not prove engineering maturity.

## Design Principles

- Lead with team capability, not community scale.
- Make the brand feel engineered: structured, readable, deliberate, and production-minded.
- Use flat surfaces and purposeful motion that supports the content rather than competing with it.
- Keep every section purposeful: explain what the team does, who does it, how to contact them, and why the work is trustworthy.
- Preserve all external social and contact paths so visitors can choose the channel that fits their intent.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard-accessible navigation. Respect reduced-motion preferences for all animation. Maintain readable text on tinted dark surfaces and avoid relying on color alone for interactive state.
